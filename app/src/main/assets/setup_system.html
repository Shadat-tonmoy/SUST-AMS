Add a course....

In order to add a course in the "Setup System" screen press the "+" Button highlighted in the image.
Fig - Add Course Button After pressing the "+" Button you will see a screen with a form for the course information. After filling the form with suitable information press the "✓" Button highlighted in the image. Note that All the fields are required here. Without filling this form you can't go further.
Fig - Add Course Screen Once you pressed the "✓" Button you redirect to the previous screen with a "New Course" added with the existing course list. Following these simple steps you can easily add a new course to your record. Once you add a new course you can Edit the course or even can Delete the course. Edit a course....

In order to edit a course in the "Setup System" screen press the ": (More Option)" Button highlighted in the image.
Fig - Course Edit Option Button After pressing the ": (More Option)" Button you will see a dialog (Sliding From Bottom of Screen) with "Edit" option (Highlighted in the image). Pressing the "Edit" option will show you another screen with a form for the necessary information for editing the course. After re-filling the form with suitable information press the "✓" Button highlighted in the image. Here also, All the fields are required here. Without filling this form you can't go further.
Fig - Edit Course Screen Once you pressed the "✓" Button you redirect to the previous screen with a "Updated Course" updated with the new information provided by you.
Fig - Updated Course Here note that Only one course is highlighted in "Blue Icon" This indicates that the highlighted course is updated with new information. These are the simple steps that you can follow to update the information of a existing course in your records. Besides editing you can also Delete the course

Remove a course....

In order to remove a course from your record in the "Setup System" screen press the ": (More Option)" Button highlighted in the image.
Fig - Course Remove Option Button After pressing the ": (More Option)" Button you will see a dialog (Sliding From Bottom of Screen) with "Delete" option (Highlighted in the image). Pressing the "Delete" option will show you another screen with a form for the necessary information for editing the course. After re-filling the form with suitable information press the "✓" Button highlighted in the image. Here also, All the fields are required here. Without filling this form you can't go further.
Fig - Delete Course Menu Once you pressed the "Delete Menu" you will prompt a confirmation dialog. Once you press the "Yes" button in the dialog all the course related record will be deleted permanently. Note that Once you press the "Yes" button all the information related to that course like All Class Instance, Student Data and Attendance Record will be permanently deleted and you will not be able to recover those data provided by you.
Fig - Delete confirmation dialog After pressing the "Yes" button in the dialog the dialog will be closed and the selected course will be erased from the "Course List" as well as from the database of the Application. Delete Operation may sometimes cause you to loose your important data. Be careful while performing a delete operation.

Import Student List From Spread Sheet

In order to add students for a course from your record in the "Setup System" screen you need to select the particular course and then press the "+" Button highlighted in the image.
Fig - Add Student Icon After pressing the "+" Button you will see some option (Pop Up) for adding student. Choose the "Import From Google Sheet" option (Highlighted in the image). After pressing the button you will need to provide Google Account Credential ("First time only") After providing the account information you will find a screen with the list of all SpreadSheet available in your Google Drive/Google Sheet The screen will look like the image aside.
Fig - Spread Sheet List To choose a SpreadSheet just press on the "SpreadSheet Icon". Then you will find all the student list from that SpreadSheet. Please Note That, Only predefined and formatted SpreadSheet can be parsed by the App. The following image shows the "Required Format" of the SpreadSheet to parse data. Any other formatted spreadsheet will not parsed correctly and will result in a wrong output.
Fig - SpreadSheet Format As the image suggests the "1A" cell must be "Name","1B" cell must be "Registration" and "1C" cell must be "Regular"
Name - All the cell below this cell will represent the "Name" of a student.
Registration - All the cell below this cell will represent the "Registration Number" of a student.
Regular - All the cell below this cell will represent the "Regularity" of a student. The values in the cell must be either "0" or "1". Here "1" indicates that the student is registered with the current course taking batch i.e. the student is regular and "0" indicates that the student is not regular i.e. "Dropper"
